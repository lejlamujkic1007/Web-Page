A QUICK LOOK BACK TO TAKE A STEP FORWARD

There is no future without the past. This is why we’ve been thinking back to our origins - when we not only developed new fashion design ideas, but new techniques as well. Our concept was to produce an exclusive capsule collection that exceeded limits in terms of development and finish, as well as to combine the retrospective with the visionary. Today, our “Vision of the Legend” is a strictly limited edition which honours our roots, and is at the same time, innovative and forward-looking.
